Project Title: Adidas_Sales_Analysis

Description: This project presents an in-depth analysis of Adidas sales performance across multiple regions in the United States. Using Power BI, the dashboard visualizes key business metrics such as total sales, profit, units sold, top-performing products, and sales trends to help stakeholders make informed decisions.

Source: Kaggle

Project Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance
Identify best-selling and underperforming products
Evaluate regional and state-level sales distribution
Understand customer purchasing behavior across sales channels (Online, Outlet, Wholesale)
Provide insights for data-driven decision making

Top 5 Key Insights
Highest Revenue Performance
Adidas recorded $900M in total sales, indicating strong demand across the country.

Most Profitable Regions
The West and Northeast regions generated the highest total sales, showing strong customer concentration in these markets.

Top Performing Products
Men’s Street Footwear and Women’s Apparel emerged as the top-selling product categories, contributing significantly to overall revenue.

Sales Method Trends
Outlet stores accounted for the largest portion of sales, followed by online purchases, showing strong in-store presence but growing digital adoption.

Seasonal Sales Behavior
Monthly sales trends show clear peaks during certain periods, highlighting key selling seasons and potential promotional opportunities.

Tools Used
Power BI Desktop
Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
DAX (Measures & Calculations)
